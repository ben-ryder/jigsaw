  ## All colours

  Accessibility is important and all use of colour in Jigsaw must at least meet [WCAG AA
  guidelines](https://www.w3.org/TR/UNDERSTANDING-WCAG20/visual-audio-contrast-contrast.html).<br />
  All colour palettes in Jigsaw have been crafted with this in mind, but care must be taken to ensure these colours are
  used correctly when combined, especially for hover/focus styling and custom app-specific features.
