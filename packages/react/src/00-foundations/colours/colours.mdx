    # Colours
    The **slate** colour palette is the most important and is
    the main colour used for the background and interface.
    The **teal** colour palette is the next most important and is
    the main "brand" colour of the design system.

    ## Accessibility
    Accessibility is important and all use of colour in Jigsaw must at least meet [WCAG AA guidelines](https://www.w3.org/TR/UNDERSTANDING-WCAG20/visual-audio-contrast-contrast.html).
    All colour palettes in Jigsaw have been crafted with this in mind, but care must be taken to ensure these colours are used correctly when combined.
