    # Theme
    The **slate** colour palette is the most important and is the main colour used for the background and interface.<br/>
    The **teal** colour palette is the next most important and is
    the main "brand" colour of the design system.

    Accessibility is important and all use of colour in Jigsaw must at least meet [WCAG AA guidelines](https://www.w3.org/TR/UNDERSTANDING-WCAG20/visual-audio-contrast-contrast.html).<br/>
    All colour palettes in Jigsaw have been crafted with this in mind, but care must be taken to ensure these colours are used correctly when combined, especially for hover/focus styling and custom app-specific features.

    ## Contextual Colours
    "Contextual" colour variables have meaning and are used in specific situations.<br/>
    This is an attempt to separate the colour itself from its semantic purpose,
    with the intention of making it easier to override these colours in the future
    and potentially allowing for different "themes" at some point.
    You can see these colours in the tables below:
